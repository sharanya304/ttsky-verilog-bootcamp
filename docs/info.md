## How it works

The Test Pattern Generator produces predefined digital patterns (counter, toggling, fixed values) for verification. It runs synchronously with the clock and cycles through patterns using simple control logic. Outputs are driven on IO pins for easy observation.

## How to test

Apply clock and reset. After initialization, observe output patterns in simulation or waveform viewer. Verify correct sequence, timing, and reset behavior.
## External hardware

Connect outputs to LEDs or a logic analyzer. Provide clock and reset. No additional peripherals required.
